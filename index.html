About

Queen Bee is a self-described “fashion-punk” band formed in Kobe, Japan, in 2009. Since then, eight of their albums have hit the Japan Top 100 Billboards and their music has been featured in anime such as Chainsaw Man and Oshi no Ko.

AVU-CHAN is the lead vocals and second guitar, along with being the lyricist and composer of Queen Bee’s songs.

YASHI-CHAN is the bass guitarist.

HIBARI-KUN is the lead guitarist.
